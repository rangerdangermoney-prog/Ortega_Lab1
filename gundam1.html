Why I Love Gundam

The Gundam franchise began with Mobile Suit Gundam (1979) and changed the mecha genre forever by focusing on war realism, politics, and character development.

Unlike typical giant robot shows, Gundam treats its Mobile Suits as military weapons rather than superheroes. This makes the stories deeper and more intense.

My Thoughts on Gunpla

Building Gunpla is more than putting plastic pieces together. It teaches patience, attention to detail, and creativity.

What really frustrates me though is how fast limited edition kits sell out. Scalpers often buy them in bulk and resell them at extremely ridiculous prices. It takes away from real fans who just want to build and enjoy the hobby.

Even with this frustration, the process of building, panel lining, and customizing a kit makes it completely worth it. There’s nothing better than finishing a build and seeing it displayed on your shelf.
